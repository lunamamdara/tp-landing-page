Proyect ACAP

As a brand we want to emphasize and promote the care of the environment through small habits that little by little can change the planet. We don't want our tote bags to be a trend but a new way of living and generating a change in our environment. Each tote bag will have in it a leaflet that will talk about recycling in general where is also describe the project that Santo Tómas de Aquino 5th year is doing for ACAP related to compost and recycling at the school
Designed by Sofía Tuñas, Salvador Magneres and Luna Mandará
