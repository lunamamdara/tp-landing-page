Our Totebags

Hi, we are EcoTotesDesign, come and discover us. We are students from the school Santo Tomas de Aquino and we want to contribute to this important topic that is our planet. The time has come to start doing something, and this depends only on us. You can be an example with us and make the school a cleaner place without plastic bags.

Objectives

Our idea is to raise awareness about how plastic bags can contaminate and be very harmful to the environment, frequently killing, by intoxication, animals such as tortoises due to their non degradable materials used for the production. EcoTotesDesigns has arrived to solve this problem. One solution that can be useful to face this problem is a new type of bag called “tote bags” which reflects a person who is interested in the topic and wants to help the world to be just a little bit better.
